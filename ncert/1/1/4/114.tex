1.1.4.The parametric equation for AB is given by
\begin{align}
\vec{x} &= \vec{A} + k\vec{m}\\
\text{where, } \vec{m} &= \vec{B} -\vec{A}\\
&= \myvec{-4 \\ 6} -\myvec{1\\ -1}\\
&= \myvec{-5 \\7}
\end{align}

Hence we get,
\begin{align}
AB: \vec{x} = &\myvec{1\\-1} + k \myvec{-5\\7}
\end{align}

Similarly, 
\begin{align}
BC: \vec{x} = &\myvec{-4\\6} + k \myvec{1\\-11}\\
CA: \vec{x} = &\myvec{-3\\-5} + k \myvec{4\\4}
\end{align}
